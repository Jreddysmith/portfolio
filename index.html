About Me

Hello! My name is Jarred Smith and I live in North Hills, California. By day, I build high responsive websites for clients and friends. In my spare time, I work on interesting side projects, tech talks, meetups, motocross, weight lifting, love drinking coffee and exploring California. I love to stay connected with people and stay active in community events. I am also a contractor who has created projects for both residential and commercial properties.

I design websites with the focus on usability and delightful user experiences. I try to solve problems through a process of user research, prototyping, and user testing. I also build sites that are responsive on every device and user friendly. Over the past few years, I have enjoyed working with many clients that were pleased with my work.
